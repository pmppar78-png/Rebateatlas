Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 24, 2025

Overview

Rebate Atlas ("we," "our," or "us") operates a free educational website that helps US households understand energy-efficiency rebates, tax credits, and incentives. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our website.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide: When you use our AI chat assistant, you may voluntarily provide information such as your ZIP code, home type, and details about planned home improvements. This information is used solely to provide you with relevant rebate guidance during your session.

Automatically Collected Information: Like most websites, we may automatically collect certain technical information through standard web technologies, including IP addresses, browser types, referring pages, pages visited, and timestamps. This helps us understand how visitors use our site and improve our service.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide personalized rebate guidance through our AI assistant
  • Improve and optimize our website and services
  • Analyze site usage and performance
  • Comply with legal obligations

Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may share information only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service Providers: We may share information with trusted third-party service providers who help us operate our website (such as hosting providers and AI service providers). These providers are contractually obligated to protect your information.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required by law or in response to valid legal requests.
  • Partner Referrals: If you click on a partner link and choose to use that partner's service, you will be subject to that partner's privacy policy. We do not share your personal information with partners unless you actively choose to engage with them.

Affiliate Relationships

Our website includes affiliate links to trusted partner services. When you click these links and use a partner's service, we may earn a referral commission at no additional cost to you. These affiliate relationships help us provide free educational resources. All affiliate links are clearly marked with appropriate disclosures.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We may use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience and analyze site usage. Most web browsers automatically accept cookies, but you can usually modify your browser settings to decline cookies if you prefer. Please note that disabling cookies may limit your ability to use certain features of our website.

Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no internet transmission is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Children's Privacy

Our website is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

Third-Party Links

Our website contains links to third-party websites, including partner services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of these external sites. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party sites you visit.

Your Choices

You have choices regarding your information:

  • You can choose not to provide certain information, though this may limit your ability to receive personalized guidance.
  • You can disable cookies through your browser settings.
  • You can choose not to click on affiliate or partner links.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect your information.
