About Rebate Atlas

Rebate Atlas helps households discover incentives that lower the cost of energy upgrades and everyday products. Type your ZIP, and we show federal, state, utility, and city programs in one place.

Our mission

We believe every household should be able to access the financial incentives they're entitled to. Federal programs like the Inflation Reduction Act created billions in tax credits and rebates, but most homeowners don't know what they qualify for or how to claim them. Rebate Atlas was built to solve that problem.

How we work

  • Aggregate incentive data from federal agencies, state energy offices, and local utilities
  • Use AI to translate program details into plain language tailored to your situation
  • Show key facts: amounts, caps, categories, and deadlines
  • Link directly to the official source on every card

Editorial standards

Information changes. We make every effort to provide accurate, up-to-date information about rebates and incentives. We encourage every user to verify details with the official source before purchase or installation.

How we earn revenue

Rebate Atlas is free for every visitor. Some pages and AI responses contain affiliate links to trusted partners. If you use one, we may receive a referral commission at no extra cost to you. This never affects the guidance we provide. All affiliate links are marked with rel="sponsored" for transparency. See our privacy policy for details.
