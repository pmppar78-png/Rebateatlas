Frequently Asked Questions

Is Rebate Atlas free?

Yes. It’s free to use.

Where does the information come from?

From official federal, state, utility, and city program pages, which we link on each card.

How accurate is the information?

We aim for accuracy but programs change. Always confirm details on the official source page.

Do you provide installation?

No. We link to third-party marketplaces to request quotes from local installers.
